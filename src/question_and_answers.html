RECRUITMENT 2021 FAQs

What is AkiraChix?

>

<

AkiraChix is a social enterprise that offers young women hands-on technical training in market-relevant technology, and life skills, to positively impact their lives and communities and close the gender gap in the technology field.
 
Where is AkiraChix located?

>

<

AkiraChix Campus is located in the secure upmarket neighbourhood of Karen in Nairobi, Kenya’s capital city.
 
What is the codeHive program?

>

<

codeHive is the AkiraChix signature program.  

  • It is a 1-year full scholarship and residency at AkiraChix Campus in Nairobi, Kenya
  • We recruit highly motivated young women from across Africa’s underserved communities
  • We offer market-ready technology, life skills and leadership training
  • Our students receive mentorship from global names in technology and business
  • Students go on a three-month work experience
  • Students attend and network at leading technology conferences and workshops
  • Students produce market-ready prototype solutions to graduate
  • Students receive support for work placement
 
Is there an age limit to who can apply?

>

<

Yes, you need to be between the ages of 19 and 24 years in January 2022
 
Is the codeHive program only for women?

>

<

Yes, we believe that it is essential to bridge the gender gap in technology by providing women with an opportunity to acquire skills in technology to make an impact. This is why we are specifically focused on young women aged 19 to 24 in our codeHive program.
 
When is the deadline for the codeHive 2021 application?

>

<

The deadline for the codeHive Class of 2022 application is 15th October 2021.
 
What does the sponsorship cover?

>

<

  • One-year tuition-free learning
  • Free accommodation, extracurricular activities, meals, and personal amenities. 
  • Training facilities including high-speed internet, biometric security control, and recreational centres
  • Access to technology hubs and maker spaces in Nairobi for tech events and prototyping respectively
  • Access to a selected pool of mentors, for career advice and personal growth.
  • Access to 6 months incubation after the one-year codeHive program dedicated to finding and securing employment for codeHive graduates
 
Where will I stay during the year of the program?

>

<

You will live on the AkiraChix campus in Nairobi, Kenya. The Campus is situated in the upmarket neighbourhood of Karen in a secure compound.
 
Am I required to have a background in technology to apply?

>

<

No, you do not. You simply need a high school certification and an eagerness to learn something new.
 
Can I apply if I am enrolled in a university program?

>

<

No, this program is specifically for those who are challenged financially in advancing their education. And have completed their high school education.
 
Can I apply if I am from outside of the three listed countries of Kenya, Rwanda, Uganda and Tanzania?

>

<

Not in 2021. As AkiraChix expands it intends to open applications to young women from across other African countries. Keep visiting our website to keep track of our expansion.
 
What additional documentation do you require with my application?

>

<

 A scanned copy of your birth certificate, your national ID, stamped letter of recommendation, and High School result slip.
 
Who should I contact for my recommendation letter?

>

<

The recommendation letter should be written by someone who knows you and is familiar with your contribution as a leader in your community. It can be drafted by your mentor, a religious leader in your community or any other trusted community leader. Your relatives, siblings or parents are not suitable neutral and objective recommendations for your application.
 
Will I be able to go home for the holidays during the program?

>

<

Yes, based on our calendar, we will take two breaks in the year to allow our students to go home and spend time with their families
 
Do I require an East African student pass for the program?

>

<

All successful candidates shortlisted for the 2022 codeHive program will receive an East African Student Pass covered by AkiraChix.
 
Do I need a passport?

>

<

Yes, you do need to have a valid passport if you are not a Kenyan citizen, The passport should be at least 1 year from expiration. (Note: not a temporary passport)
 
Do I need to have a COVID-19 test done before I leave the country?

>

<

Yes, all successful candidates residing outside of Kenya’s borders are required to undergo a COVID-19 test. And have a Ministry of Health certified PCR COVID-19 test certificate, which will be presented at the point of entry.
 
Will I be required to undergo quarantine on arrival at the AkiraChix Campus?

>

<

All successful candidates will be required to undergo quarantine for 14 days on the AkiraChix campus, following which they will then undergo a COVID-19 test.
 
What happens if I test COVID-19 positive after quarantine on campus?

>

<

You will be placed in an isolation room on campus and receive treatment at the cost of the institution.
 
Who will cover my transport costs to Nairobi?

>

<

AkiraChix will cover all your costs once on campus, travel to campus from your respective country and region of origin is at the student’s cost.
 
What happens if I am shortlisted for the 2022 codeHive program, and my country borders are not open,
how do I get to the AkiraChix Campus?

>

<

AkiraChix will work with you to coordinate a viable and legal option for transportation from your home country to the AkiraChix campus in Nairobi.
